About Me

I'm a PhD researcher with a background in art, philosophy and sociology. I've been a professional illustrator since 2011, and I've completed an MA in Language and Cognition at King's College London and an MA in Sociology at Bielefeld University in Germany.

I am interested in the way people communicate meaning through visual markings and notation systems, and am beginning to research this topic by creating novel devices that incorporate drawings and comics to test theories of cognition. I have also published work in social epistemology and social boundaries with regards to immigration.
